# States and State Regions in Victoria III

A **state region** is a static portion of a the physical map. For example, a state region of *STATE_GRANADA*.

A **state** is a portion of a region controlled by a country. For example, if Spain controls a portion of *STATE_GRANADA*, and Britain controls the other. Then there will be two states inside the state region: *Spanish STATE_GRANADA* and *British STATE_GRANADA*.
