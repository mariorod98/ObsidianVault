# General Abilities
These abilities are used in [[Cthulhu Confidential - Challenges|Challenges]] and [[Cthulhu Confidential - Quick tests|Quick tests]] to determine whether you succeed or fail when traying to take actions other than gathering information.

For each General Ability, the player has a level that ranges from 1 to 3. The level determines the number of dices you will use in a [[Cthulhu Confidential - Challenges|Challenges]].

## Type of general abilities
General abilities can be divided into:
- **Manual**, drawing on a combination of skill training and fine motor skills.
- **Mental**, drawing on mostly intellectual study, perception, thought and memory.
- **Physical**, using your gross motor skills.

The list of abilities is:
- **Athletics (Physical)**. 
	- Perform general acts of physical strength (run, jump, throw, doge,...).
	- Resist effects of disease, poisoning and intoxicants.
	- Resist effects of exposure (hypothermia, frostbite, sunstroke,...).
- **Conceal (Manual)**.
	- Hide things from view. Camuflage, holding items on you, sneaking things into a drawer or pocket, build a secret compartiment, alter an object's visual signatur with paint.
	- Discover items that have been concealed as long as it is not hidden information (use Evidence Collection for that).
- **Cool (Mental)**. Ability to not take an impulsive action and *act cool*. It saves you from:
	- Falling for the wrong kind of dame.
	- Showing a tell that warns the killer that you know he did it.
	- Doing drugs/loot games when you shouldnÂ´t.
	- Resist supernatural forces attempting to influence your behaviour.
- **Devices (Manual)**. 
	- Build, repair, disassemble and operate electrical or mechanical devices.
	- Hot-wire cars, alarms...
	- Create jury-rigged devices from scrap.
	- Doubles as an Investigative Ability when used to determine the function of an equipment, read Morse Code, tap a telephone, evaluate quality of workmanship...
- **Disguise (Manual)**. Become someone els: clothjes, voice, mannerisms and posture.
	- Imnpersonate someone on the phone.
	- Blend into a crowd.
	- Mingle at a party you were not invited to.
	- Hold an entire conversation with an unwitting friend or suspect.
- **Driving (Manual)**. Skilled defensive driver, capable of wringing high performance from even the most recalcitrant automobile, pickup truck or bus. You an:
	- evade or conduct pursuit.
	- Avoid collisions or minimize the damage.
	- navigate, read maps and maintain a sense of direction.
	- Drive cars, trucks, motorcycles, small motor boats.
	- Once per case, reveal that you can drive vehicles with specialized training (bus, yatch, plane, tank, helicopter...). You have to describe how you learned it.
